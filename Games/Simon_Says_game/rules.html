Simon Game Rules:

  1. Simon will show a sequence of colors (lights or sounds) in a specific pattern.
  2. Remember and repeat the pattern shown by Simon by clicking or pressing the corresponding color buttons.
  3. The pattern will start with just one color. After successfully repeating the pattern, Simon will add one more color to the sequence in the next turn.
  4. The game continues to add one more color to the pattern with each successful repetition.
  5. If you make a mistake and fail to repeat the correct pattern, the game will end, and your score will be displayed.
  6. Pay close attention to the growing pattern and stay focused as the game becomes more challenging.
  7. Challenge yourself to beat your high score and reach the highest level possible.
  8. Have fun and enjoy the game!
  9. Remember, the Simon game gets progressively harder as you advance through the levels, so be prepared for an exciting and memory-testing experience!

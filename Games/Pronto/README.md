# **Pronto** 

---

<br>

## **Description 📃**
- A very simple reaction game about not getting off the line. Works on your mobile too. 
- But first, your mission is to dodge the way, or zap them with your booster.

## **How to play? 🕹️**
- Controls:
	- Move - W, A, S, D (or) Arrow Keys

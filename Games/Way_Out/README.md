# **Way_Out** 

---

<br>

## **Description 📃**
- The door is locked. 
- Way Out is a platformer game.

## **How to play? 🕹️**
- Instructions:
	- You'll have to explore a dangerous dungeon full of traps and monsters and find 4 lost gems to open the door and get your WAY OUT!
- Controls:
	- LEFT/RIGHT - Move
	- UP - Jump

## **Description 📃**

- The game is based on the game of rock paper and scissors.

## **functionalities 🎮**
<!-- add functionalities over here -->
- Here the user clicks one of the buttons available and the computer does the same which is generated randomly. According to both of their choices, one of the both wins or either its a tie.
<br>

## **How to play? 🕹️**
<!-- add the steps how to play games -->
- In this,the user chooses one from the three buttons available and the computer does it randomly.Three conditions may occur i.e
1. Rock defeats Scissor
2. Scissor defeats Paper
3. Paper defeats Rock

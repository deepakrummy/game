# **Black_Hole_Square** 

---

<br>

## **Description 📃**
- Welcome to Black Hole Square!
- Clean up all the other squares in the universe. Just tap them and see what happens.

## **How to play? 🕹️**
- Controls:
    - `R` for retry a level
    - `M` for mute sound
    - Swipe right to reset a puzzle or go back to the old ones. Swipe left to skip a puzzle.
